PRP KIT Test Results

Tested by Green Cross Reference Lab

Our kit is manufactured at a certified ISO 13485 FDA approved factory in Korea. In an effort to validate the results from
the PRP Kit we contracted with Green Cross Reference Labs, a company that has been providing independent
laboratory results for over 25 years through 30 branches across the country.

Ten patients, 5 men and 5 women, ranging from 28 to 75 years of age, each gave 3 samples. Each sample (3cc of PRP)
was tested utilizing the same methodology based on the industry’s standard of care. The results are depicted below and
clearly show a significant improvement in the per μL concentration utilizing the PRP Kit.
