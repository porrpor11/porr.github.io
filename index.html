Platelet Rich Plasma

The PRP treatment is quick, essentially painless and harnesses the body’s natural healing abilities without surgery or drugs. The goal of the treatment is intentional, targeted inflammation at a point of injury. PRP injections are often painless and there is little to no risk of rejection. The result is stimulation of growth factors, attraction of stem cells and instigation of the healing cascade.

PRP Therapy and PRP Treatments have been shown to be effective in combination with Radio-Frequency, IPL, ablative and non-ablative laser.
